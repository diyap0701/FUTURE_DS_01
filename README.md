## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes business sales data to identify key revenue trends, top-performing products, profitable categories, and regional sales performance. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can help decision-makers improve sales strategies and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare raw sales data.
- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Compare sales across different categories.
- Evaluate regional sales performance.
- Generate business insights and actionable recommendations.

## 📈 Key Findings

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales fluctuate across different months.
- Sales generally increase towards the end of the observed period.
- Seasonal demand patterns can help optimize inventory planning.

### 📦 Sales by Category
- Technology generated the highest overall sales.
- Furniture ranked second in sales.
- Office Supplies also contributed significantly to total revenue.

### 💰 Profit by Category
- Technology is the most profitable category.
- Office Supplies generated strong profits.
- Furniture showed comparatively lower profitability despite strong sales.

### 🌎 Regional Performance
- The West region recorded the highest sales.
- The East region followed closely.
- The South region generated the lowest sales.

### 🏆 Top Selling Products
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the highest-selling product.
- Technology products dominate the list of top-selling products.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing investment in the Technology category.
- Review pricing and cost structure for Furniture products to improve profit margins.
- Expand promotional campaigns in the South region to improve sales performance.
- Maintain sufficient inventory for top-selling products.
- Use historical sales trends for demand forecasting and inventory planning.
